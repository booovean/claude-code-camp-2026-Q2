# World Knowledge & Map

## Known Locations

### Midgaard City
- **The Tournament And Practice Yard**
  - **Exits:** North (`n` to Bar Of Swordsmen), Down (`d` into darkness/well)
  - **NPCs:** Guildmaster (sharpening an axe)

- **The Bar Of Swordsmen**
  - **Exits:** South (`s` to Practice Yard), West (`w` to Entrance Hall)

- **The Entrance Hall To The Guild Of Swordsmen**
  - **Exits:** East (`e` to Bar), North (`n` to Main Street)

- **Main Street & Market Square**
  - **Exits:** Links to General Store, Weapon Shop, Pet Shop, Bakery, and Temple Square to the North.

- **The Temple Of Midgaard**
  - **Exits:** North (`n` to Temple Altar), East (`e` to Donation Room)
  - **Donation Room:** Can get a free candle (`light source`).

### Newbie Zone & Path to Minotaur
- **Path from Midgaard Temple:**
  1. From Temple Altar, go `n` (Behind The Temple Altar).
  2. Go `n`, `n` through **The Great Field Of Midgaard**.
  3. Go `e` into **The Entrance To The Newbie Zone**.
  4. Go `n` into **The Beginning Of The Passage**.
  5. Go `e` into **The Dirty Hallway**, open south door (`open door south`), go `s` into **A Small Room**.
  6. Open east door (`open door east`), go `e` into **More Of The Hallway**.
  7. Go `s` into **Another Corner**, open east door (`open door east`), go `e` into **The Alchemist's Room**.
  8. Go down (`d`) the dark stairs into **The Entrance** (Dungeon Level).
  9. Go `n` into **A Crossing Of Corridors**.
  10. Go `n` into **A Corner In The Hallway**.
  11. Go `e` into **Another Turn**.

### Target Boss Location: The Great Minotaur
- **Location:** **Another Turn** (Dungeon Level of Newbie Zone)
- **Boss:** **The Great Minotaur**
  - **Description:** A massive man with the head of a bull.
  - **Equipment Worn:** `the smelly hide of the Minotaur`, `the horns of the Minotaur`.
  - **Warning Sign:** Alchemist's Room sign warns: *"If you are below level 7 and alone, or below level 4 then bugger off! Or else don't blame me if you die..."*
